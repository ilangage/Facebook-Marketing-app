Data Deletion Instructions

How to request removal of data associated with this application.

Overview

You can ask us to delete or stop using personal data processed through this App (subject to legal or technical limits—for example data we must keep for security or compliance, or data held only by Meta on Meta’s systems).

Through your Facebook or Meta account

If you granted this App access via Facebook or Meta, you can review or remove the App from your account using Meta’s settings (for example Settings & privacy → Settings → Apps and websites on Facebook, or the equivalent in Accounts Center). Meta explains how in its Help Center. Removing the App may stop future data sharing; it does not always delete data we already received. For deletion from our systems, use email or the steps below.

How to request deletion from us

We process data deletion requests promptly, in line with our obligations under the Meta Platform Terms and applicable law. Send an email to:

kanaayams@gmail.com

Use the subject line “Data deletion request” so we can route your message quickly.

What to include

Please include enough detail for us to find your data, such as:

  • The email address or account identifier you use with the App or with our team;
  • Your business or project name as shown in the App (if applicable);
  • A clear statement that you want your data deleted or that you withdraw consent for processing;
  • Any relevant dates or screenshots (optional) if you are following up on a specific issue.

What we will do

After we receive your request we will:

  • Confirm receipt where appropriate;
  • Verify that the request relates to data we control in this App or our systems;
  • Delete or anonymize that data without undue delay where possible, or explain any limitation (for example data held only inside Meta must be addressed using Meta’s tools or policies);
  • Respond within a reasonable time, typically within 30 days for straightforward requests, unless a longer period is required by volume or complexity.

If this App is configured with Meta’s Data Deletion Request callback URL in the Meta App Dashboard, Meta may also notify our server when a user removes the App; we still honor email requests sent to the address above.

Meta and ad accounts

Campaigns, ads, and audiences stored in Meta are subject to Meta’s tools and policies. Deleting data in our App does not automatically delete your Meta ad account or objects inside Meta; you may need to use Meta Business settings for that.

Related

For how we handle data in general, see our Privacy Policy. For rules of use, see Terms of Service.
