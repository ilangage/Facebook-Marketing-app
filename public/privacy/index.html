Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026. This policy describes how we handle information for this application.

Who we are

This policy applies to the Facebook Marketing / Bot Ops web application (the “App”) used to manage advertising workflows, campaign tooling, and related analytics for authorized business use.

What the App does

The App provides a console for operations such as viewing dashboard state, managing campaign and creative workflows, audience and CRM-related features, automation, and—when you configure it—calls to the Meta Marketing API. The exact features available depend on your deployment and whether you use live Meta credentials or mock / test mode.

Meta Platform Terms

Where this App uses Meta products (for example the Marketing API, ad accounts, or related permissions), we use those products in line with the Meta Platform Terms, Developer Policies, and other applicable Meta terms and product policies. Nothing in this policy limits Meta’s own terms or your obligations when using Meta.

Information we collect or process

We collect or process information only as needed to run the App. This may include data you provide directly, data we receive from Meta when you authorize access (subject to the permissions and tokens you grant), and basic technical data as described below.

  • Account and access data — for example API keys or access tokens you configure (stored according to your hosting environment), identifiers needed to authenticate requests, and basic technical logs (such as timestamps and error messages).
  • Data from Meta (when you connect Meta) — depending on your permissions and use of the App, this may include identifiers and metadata related to your Meta ad account, campaigns, ad sets, ads, creatives, insights or performance metrics, audience or custom audience operations, page or asset identifiers, and other fields returned by the Meta APIs you enable. Meta processes much of this on Meta’s systems; we process what our App and backend store or display to provide the service.
  • Advertising, CRM, and operational data — such as campaign names, ad set settings, creative copy or asset references, funnel or CRM-related fields, and similar data you enter or sync through the App.
  • Technical and usage data — for example server or application logs, IP addresses, and device or browser information as typically logged by hosting infrastructure, to secure and operate the service.
  • Support communications — information you send when you contact us (for example by email).

We do not sell personal data. We do not use Meta user data for purposes prohibited by Meta’s policies.

How we use information (purposes)

We use information only for purposes such as:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving the App and its features;
  • Displaying dashboards, analytics, and reporting you request;
  • Communicating with you about support, security, or policy updates;
  • Responding to privacy rights and data-deletion requests;
  • Complying with law and enforcing our terms.

Meta and other third parties

When you connect Meta, Meta also processes data under Meta’s own policies and terms. We do not control Meta’s systems. See Meta’s resources for how Meta handles account, advertising, and related data. Other third-party services you connect may have their own terms.

How to request deletion of your data

Meta expects developers to offer a clear way for people to request deletion of data the App has obtained from or through Meta or the developer. You may:

  • Email us at kanaayams@gmail.com with a data deletion request; or
  • Follow the instructions on our dedicated page: Data deletion instructions.
  • If you connected this App using Facebook or Meta login, you can also review or remove the App from your Facebook/Meta account settings (for example under Apps and websites or connected experiences, as Meta describes in its Help Center). Removing the App there may not delete all data we already hold; use email or the data deletion page if you need a full deletion from our systems.

Retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law. Retention depends on your backend storage (for example SQLite or hosting logs). You may request deletion as described on our Data deletion page.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data in line with the nature of the service. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; use strong credentials and follow Meta’s security recommendations for tokens and ad accounts.

Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data, or to object to certain processing. To request deletion, use the options in How to request deletion of your data above. For other requests or questions, contact us at the email below.

Children

The App is intended for business users and is not directed at children under 16.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and revise the “Last updated” date when we do.
